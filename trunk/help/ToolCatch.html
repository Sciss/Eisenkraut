Catch Mode

When catch mode is active (keyboard V), the timeline view will automatically advance, just before the timeline position moves outside the window in playback (what is called 'Page Scroll during Playback' in ProTools for example) or timeline-drag. Dragging the timeline scroll bar disables catch if the drag is released and the timeline position is outside the view. When catch "looses track" of the timeline position, simply re-active it by pressing V twice.
