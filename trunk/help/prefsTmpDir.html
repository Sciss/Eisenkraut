General Prefs : Temporary Folder

The application creates a couple of temporary files which will be deleted automatically when a session is closed or the program is quit. On Un*x systems, they should go to "/var/tmp", but you may want to choose a folder on a different harddisk if your system partition is small, for example.

Eisenkraut currently has no means to determine the free space in the temporary folder, so be sure to point it to a harddisk partition that is big enough to hold all the edits you wish to perform. As a rule of thumb, a monophonic sound at 44.1 kHz takes around 20 MB per minute (since all edits are performed in 32bit floating point format).
