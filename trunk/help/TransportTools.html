Transport Tools

The transport tools control realtime playback of the session. From left to right you see the Rewind, Stop, Play, Fast Forward button, the Loop. Keyboard shortcuts are Space or Keypad-0 for Play/Stop, Numpad-/ for Loop. Rewind and Fast Forward will have shortcuts in the next version. Shift + Space will play back at half speed, Shift + Alt + Space will play back at double speed.

Loop playback: the loop playback uses the current timeline selection and can be altered using the timeline axis, the timeline pointer tool or the observer time tab.
