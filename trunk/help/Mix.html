Mix

Adds audio content from the clipboard to the selected region. The volume of the original content and the volume of the added clipboard content can be adjusted independantly. A separate switch allows phase inversion.

Application examples:

  • 0% (-inf dB) dry gain, and 100% (0 dB) mix/clipboard gain : replaces the original content by the clipboard content
  • 100% dry gain, and 100% mix/clipboard gain, inverted clipboard phase : subtracts the clipboard content from the current content
  • 50% (-6 dB) dry gain, and 50% (-6 dB) mix/clipboard gain : mixes both contents with equal volume, prevents clipping
